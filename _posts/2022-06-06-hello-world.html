Net Zero by 2050

Expanding renewables, electrification, energy efficiency and emission reduction will only bring us part-way: To achieve net zero by 2050, we need to decarbonise all industries. This means building a circular carbon economy where carbon dioxide is used as a building block to make useful chemicals and fuels. However, the technology is not there yet.

Dunia's mission

We have named the company 'Dunia Innovations' (Dunia from Arabic دُنْيا stands for ‘World’) as we strive for impact and want to develop the innovations the world needs the most.

Have you ever wondered about the carbon footprint you are leaving daily?

From buying your favourite clothes to surfing the web, all our actions carry a carbon footprint. Imagine a carbon-neutral world where our actions do not damage the planet we live on.

Day-to-day products made from CO2

What is holding us back from utilising CO2 to its full potential are inefficient catalysts.

Current bottleneck in carbon utilisation is inefficient catalysts.

Current innovation in catalysis has not changed much in the past century and with the current paradigm it will be too late for our climate targets. Dunia aims to disrupt catalyst discovery by leveraging the 21st century AI and robotic tools. Our platform technology supercharges the catalyst innovation process, reducing time and cost to market by up to 90%. More importantly, our approach has the potential to enable GHGs emission reduction on a gigaton scale per annum.

The dreams of yesterday are the hopes of today and the reality of tomorrow.
